Risk Management

  1. Cybersecurity: IT's role in cybersecurity goes beyond prevention. Continuous monitoring, incident response systems, and threat intelligence are integral parts of risk management. In-depth security audits and penetration testing identify vulnerabilities.

  2. Compliance and Regulation: IT helps companies track and adhere to evolving regulations. Compliance management systems maintain records and facilitate audits, ensuring businesses meet legal requirements.

  3. Business Continuity: Disaster recovery plans leverage IT infrastructure. Regular data backups, redundancy, and cloud solutions ensure minimal downtime during disasters, reducing business risk.
