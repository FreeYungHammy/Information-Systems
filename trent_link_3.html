How can a business gain a competitive advantage?

  1. Innovation: IT fosters innovation by enabling research and development. Companies can simulate product designs, conduct virtual testing, and develop prototypes using computer-aided design (CAD) and simulation software.

  2. Efficiency: IT-driven automation and process optimization enhance efficiency. Robotic Process Automation (RPA) can streamline repetitive tasks, reducing labor costs and minimizing errors.

  3. Customer Experience: IT personalizes customer experiences. Through data analytics, companies tailor marketing, provide recommendations, and offer seamless online shopping experiences.
